## Props

### `Card`

- **BaseCard**

  - > **Inherits `BoxProps`**
  - `variant?`: elevated | filled | outlined
    - The variant theme of the card
    - Default: `"outlined"`

- **Card.Head**

  - > **Inherits `BoxProps`**
  - `header?`: string
    - The title of the card
    - Default: `undefined`
  - `subheader?`: string
    - The subtitle of the card
    - Default: `undefined`

- **Card.Image**

  - > **Inherits `BoxProps`, `vue.ImgHTMLAttributes`**

- **Card.Actions**
  - > **Inherits `BoxProps`**
  - `#sub`: slot
    - The container for actions at the left side of the card
  - `separator?`: boolean
    - Whether to add a separator at the top of the actions container
    - Default: `false`
