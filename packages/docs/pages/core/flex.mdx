## Props

### `FlexProps`

- > **Inherits `BoxProps`**
- `direction?`: 'row' | 'column' | 'row-reverse' | 'column-reverse'
  - The direction of the flex
  - Default: `undefined`
- `wrap?`: 'nowrap' | 'wrap' | 'wrap-reverse'
  - The wrap of the flex
  - Default: `undefined`
- `align?`: 'start' | 'end' | 'center' | 'between' | 'around' | 'evenly'
  - The align of the flex
  - Default: `undefined`
- `justify?`: 'start' | 'end' | 'center' | 'between' | 'around' | 'evenly'
  - The justify of the flex
  - Default: `undefined`
